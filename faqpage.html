FAQs

Why should I join?

MENU Membership Card is free to participate in and it’s easy!
The more you shop, the more you earn. It’s that simple.
With every Rupee spent on products purchasing, you earn equal points. Swipe/Scan your card with
every purchase and watch the points add up!
How do I become a member of the MENU Membership Card Offer?
You can join at any MENU outlet or online.
Does it cost anything to become a member?
There’s no fee to join and no ongoing membership fee.
Just make one qualifying purchase per calendar year to keep your membership active.
How do I change my contact information?
Visit your nearest MENU outlet or by online just sign in at ‘select’ My Account then My Profile.
Once there, you can add or update your address and other contact information.
